Welcome to Aeroservoelasticity Laboratory

The lab is currently headed by Prof Kartik Venkataraman. The lab carries out research in the areas of fluid-structure interactions with a major focus on aeroelasticity while also foraying into vascular flow mechanical problems such as aneurysms. For further details, please visit Research page.
